About Cyber Arc

Our project comprehensively analyzes and maps the cybersecurity threat landscape in Kenya, examining sources like malware attacks, phishing attempts, data breaches, and other forms of cybercrime. We aim to provide insights into the evolving cybersecurity challenges and inform the development of targeted strategies and policies to enhance cyber resilience.
